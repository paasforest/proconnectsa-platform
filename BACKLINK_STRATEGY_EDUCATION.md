## ğŸ“Š What Makes a Good Backlink?

### 1. **Domain Authority (DA)**
- **High DA (40+):** Very valuable
- **Medium DA (20-40):** Good
- **Low DA (0-20):** Less valuable

### 2. **Relevance**
- **Perfect:** Service directory, local business site
- **Good:** General business directory, news site
- **Okay:** Any legitimate website
- **Bad:** Completely unrelated (gambling, adult content, etc.)

### 3. **Context**
- **Best:** Natural mention in article content
- **Good:** Listed in directory
- **Okay:** Footer link
- **Bad:** Spam comment, paid link farm

### 4. **Anchor Text**
- **Best:** "ProConnectSA" or "service providers in South Africa"
- **Good:** "Get quotes" or "find professionals"
- **Bad:** "Click here" or "buy now"
