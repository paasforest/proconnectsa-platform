## Step-by-Step Setup

### Step 1: Go to Google Business
1. Visit: https://www.google.com/business/
2. Click "Manage now"
3. Sign in with your Google account (or create one)

### Step 2: Enter Business Information

#### Business Name
```
ProConnectSA
```

#### Business Category
**Primary Category:**
```

```

**Additional Categories:**
- Service Provider
- Local Business Directory
- Internet Service Provider

#### Business Address
```

```
**Note:** If you work from home, you can hide your address and show service area only.

**Required Photos:**

### Step 6: Add Attributes

**Select applicable:**
- [ ] Online service
- [ ] Appointment required
- [ ] Free quote available
- [ ] Verified business

### Step 7: Verify Your Business

**Verification Options:**
1. **Phone** - Google calls you with verification code
2. **Email** - Google emails you verification code
3. **Postcard** - Google mails you a postcard (takes 1-2 weeks)

**Recommended:** Phone or Email (fastest)

## Common Questions

**Q: Do I need a physical address?**
A: No, you can hide your address and show service areas only.

**Q: How long does verification take?**
A: Phone/Email: 5-10 minutes. Postcard: 1-2 weeks.

**Q: Is it free?**
A: Yes, Google Business Profile is completely free.

**Q: Can I manage multiple locations?**
A: Yes, you can add multiple locations if you have offices in different cities.
