## How to Check Your Backlinks

### Method 1: Google Search Console
1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Select your property (proconnectsa.co.za)
3. Click "Links" in the left menu
4. View "External links" to see backlinks

### Method 2: Free Tools
- **Ahrefs Backlink Checker** (free version): https://ahrefs.com/backlink-checker
- **Ubersuggest** (free): https://neilpatel.com/ubersuggest/
- **Small SEO Tools**: https://smallseotools.com/backlink-checker/

### Method 3: Paid Tools (More Accurate)
- **Ahrefs** - Most comprehensive
- **SEMrush** - Good competitor analysis
- **Moz** - Domain authority tracking

---

## How to Check Domain Authority

### Free Method: Moz Link Explorer
1. Go to https://moz.com/link-explorer
2. Enter your domain: proconnectsa.co.za
3. View Domain Authority (DA) score

### What DA Means:
- **0-20:** Low authority (your current likely range)
- **20-40:** Medium authority (target for 3-6 months)
- **40-60:** High authority (target for 6-12 months)
- **60+:** Very high authority (long-term goal)
